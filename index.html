Ceramic coating

Protection with a
deeper finish.

Ceramic coating adds a durable protective layer over properly prepared paint. It can enhance gloss, improve water behavior and make routine upkeep more manageable.

A vehicle assessment helps determine the appropriate preparation and coating service.

Frequently asked

Before your appointment.

Which detailing service should I choose?+

Choose based on the areas you want addressed and your vehicle’s condition. If you are unsure, call the shop before booking for guidance.

Do you detail SUVs and trucks?+

Yes. We offer service options for small and larger vehicles, including cars, SUVs, pickup trucks and commercial vehicles.

What should I do before an interior detail?+

Please remove valuables and personal items when possible so the team can access the areas being cleaned.

How long will my appointment take?+

Timing depends on vehicle size, condition and selected service. Check the booking page for the current estimated duration of each option.

Is ceramic coating right for every vehicle?+

Suitability and preparation depend on paint condition and your goals. Contact the shop to discuss the best approach for your vehicle.
